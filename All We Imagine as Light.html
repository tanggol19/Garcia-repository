Synopsis / Plot

Malayalis Prabha and Anu are nurses living together in Mumbai. Prabha is straitlaced and upright, and yearns for her husband who lives in Germany and only came to India once for a short arranged marriage with her, but has not called her in over a year. Anu is more outgoing and is having a secret affair with a Muslim man named Shiaz
